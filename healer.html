"疗愈不是让你变成另一个人，而是帮你回到最初的自己。" "Healing isn't about becoming someone else — it's about returning to who you already are."

赵子珊来自武汉，现居巴厘岛与新西兰之间，从小通过母亲的带领接触瑜伽，开始探索身心连结的实践。

2018 年于法国尼斯大学取得香水与调味学位，并持有日耳曼式和英式芳香疗法认证。2020 年在巴厘岛完成 175 小时 Esalen 按摩课程，并于 2022 年正式成为认证疗愈师。

2023–2024 年间，她在 Esalen 按摩课程中担任助理及中文翻译，并启动了将芳香疗法与按摩、静修相结合的综合课程项目。她 2025 年的目标是前往 Esalen Institute 接受正式的按摩教学训练。

Zhao Zishan, originally from Wuhan and now based between Bali and New Zealand, began exploring mind-body practices through yoga with her mother.

She graduated from the University of Nice, France in 2018 with a degree in perfumery, holds certifications in Germanic and English-style aromatherapy, and became Esalen-certified in 2022.

Between 2023–2024, she worked as assistant and Chinese translator for Esalen Massage courses, launching programs combining aromatherapy, massage and retreats. Her 2025 goal is to train as an Esalen Massage instructor at the Esalen Institute.
